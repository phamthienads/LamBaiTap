Selected Story: Little Red Riding Hood

The story is changed with more challenges

The player plays the role of controlling Little Red Riding Hood to overcome challenges

The player's ultimate goal is to choose the right thing to use to win the challenges and defeat the final boss

"Computer" has the role of giving instructions and implementing a random element in the game

The challenges that must be overcome are:
The Forest
The Village
The Cave
The Ice River
The Castle
The City
The Desert
The Island
The Final Boss

And in more like 1 map than different levels

Players need to overcome small challenges by choosing the right items to defeat and receive rewards, collecting at least 5 items to defeat the final boss.
